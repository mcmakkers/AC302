Lesson 1- My Gallery of DAWs

Live, by Ableton

Ableton's unique factor is that it has a "session view", a way to record clips, then rearrange them on-the-go to make arrangements.

Logic Pro X, by Apple

This macOS-exclusive DAW is the leading competitior to Live. The full version is many hundred pounds cheaper than Ableton's, however this is probably because Apple devices are overpriced.

GarageBand, by Apple

This beginner-friendly DAW, which comes installed on every Apple device, is very likely the first DAW you have used. However, its lack of effects make it unviable to use professionally
